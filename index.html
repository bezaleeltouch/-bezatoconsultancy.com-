Common Questions

Frequently Asked Questions

How long are the courses?+
Beginner and Intermediate courses are 4 weeks each. The advanced Machine Learning & Automation course runs for 6 weeks. All courses include live sessions, projects, and weekly feedback from the trainer.
Can I attend online?+
Yes. All courses are fully available online via live interactive sessions — starting July 2025. In-person classes run at our Dopemu, Lagos campus from August 2025, with guaranteed power supply and free data.
Do I get a certificate?+
Yes. Every student who completes the course and passes the assessment receives a BezaTo Consultancy certificate of completion. Graduates who pass the readiness assessment also unlock the Career Launchpad bundle — CV, LinkedIn, and freelance profile setup — free.
What are the payment options?+
We accept full payment or structured installments. WhatsApp us to discuss a payment plan that works for your budget. Multi-course bundle enrolments also come with additional discounts on total tuition.
Do you offer private or corporate training?+
Yes. BezaTo offers private 1-on-1 sessions, group corporate training, and in-company seminars for Marketing, HR, and Sales analytics teams. Contact us to get a custom proposal and pricing.
I'm a complete beginner — can I enrol?+
Absolutely. Our Beginner tracks — Excel, Power BI, and SPSS — require zero prior experience. We also offer a free consultation to help you identify the best course for your background and career goals.
What is the consultancy assessment?+
After completing your course, you take a practical assessment to demonstrate you can apply what you've learned. Passing unlocks your Career Launchpad (CV, LinkedIn, freelance setup) and qualifies you for our job placement assistance. This ensures only serious, job-ready graduates enter the support programme.
